Early Life

My passion for web development began in 1998 when I first started learning HTML. I was fascinated by how a few lines of code could shape a webpage, and I spent hours experimenting and building. In high school, I excelled in my Java class, quickly mastering the language and gaining the trust of my instructors.

This led to a rare opportunity—being given access to the entire school district's server, so that I could improve the design and functionality of various school web pages.

Growth And Development

Not long after high school, I teamed up with friends to start an eCommerce business. My role was clear—I built the website and online store, laying the foundation for our digital presence. This hands-on experience reinforced my love for web development and set me on a path to deepen my skills. Later, I pursued and earned a certification in the MERN stack, sharpening my expertise in modern web technologies and full-stack development.

Current Project Updates

I continue to grow as a developer through constant learning, using free online resources, experimentation, and real-world practice. My current focus is on Python, which I’ve integrated into my backend development workflow. Right now, I’m working on a Python web framework, pushing myself to create efficient, flexible solutions from the ground up. Each project I take on is an opportunity to refine my skills and explore new possibilities in development.
